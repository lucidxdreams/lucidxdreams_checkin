
# Privacy Policy - Lucid x Dreams

**Last Updated: January 3, 2026**
**Facility:** Lucid x Dreams | 1336 N Capitol NW, Washington, DC 20002

## 1. Introduction
Lucid x Dreams collects and processes personal information to help you submit medical cannabis applications to DC ABCA. This policy explains how we protect your data. We are NOT affiliated with ABCA.

## 2. Information We Collect

**From Your ID:**
- First name, last name
- Date of birth
- Address
- Document type

**From Your Input:**
- Phone number
- Email address
- Submission timestamp

**Automatic Data:**
- IP address
- Browser type
- Submission time
- Device information

## 3. How We Use Your Information

**Primary Uses:**
- Submit your application to DC ABCA
- Verify accuracy of extracted data
- Contact you about your submission
- Comply with legal obligations

**We DO NOT:**
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use for marketing or advertising
- Share with anyone except ABCA and Quickbase
- Use tracking cookies
- Share with federal agencies

## 4. Data Sharing

Your information is shared with:

| Entity | Purpose | Basis |
|--------|---------|-------|
| DC ABCA | Application submission | Your consent |
| Quickbase | Secure data storage | Our service provider |
| Surya/EasyOCR | OCR processing | Anonymized processing |

We do NOT share data with:
- Third-party marketers
- Data brokers
- Social media platforms
- Insurance companies
- Federal agencies

## 5. Data Security
Your data is protected by:
- HTTPS encryption (in transit)
- Secure Quickbase storage (encryption at rest)
- Access controls (staff authorization only)
- Regular backups
- Audit logging

No system is 100% secure. You submit at your own risk.

## 6. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention | Reason |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| ID Image | 90 days | Verification only |
| Application Record | 12 months | Compliance & audit |
| Consent Logs | 12 months | Legal protection |
| Email Communications | 12 months | Records |
| Server Logs | 30 days | Security |

After retention periods, data is permanently deleted.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate info
- **Deletion:** Request deletion after 90 days
- **Withdraw Consent:** Email within 24 hours to stop processing
- **No Discrimination:** We won't penalize you for exercising rights

**To exercise rights:** Email help@lucidxdreams.com with proof of identity.
**Response time:** 14 business days.

## 8. California Residents (CCPA)
You have additional rights:
- Right to know what data we collect
- Right to delete your data

- Right to non-discrimination

## 9. DC Residents
Your data is subject to DC privacy laws and ABCA regulations.
