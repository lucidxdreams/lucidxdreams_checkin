1. Service Description

Lucid x Dreams provides a time-saving form completion service that helps DC residents apply for medical cannabis cards through the DC Alcohol Beverage and Cannabis Administration (ABCA). We are NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to ABCA, Quickbase, or any government agency. We simply help you fill out and submit your application faster.

2. What We Do

  • Extract information from your government ID using OCR technology
  • Auto-fill your ABCA application form
  • Submit your completed application on your behalf
  • Provide confirmation of submission

3. What We Don't Do

  • We do NOT determine your eligibility
  • We do NOT approve or deny applications
  • We do NOT guarantee ABCA approval
  • We do NOT provide legal representation
  • We are NOT affiliated with ABCA or any government entity
